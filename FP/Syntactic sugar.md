"Syntactic sugar" just means "a more convenient syntax."

**syntactic sugar** is [syntax](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Syntax_\(programming_languages\) "Syntax (programming languages)") within a [programming language](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Programming_language "Programming language") that is designed to make things easier to read or to express. It makes the language "sweeter" for human use: things can be expressed more clearly, more concisely, or in an alternative style that some may prefer.
